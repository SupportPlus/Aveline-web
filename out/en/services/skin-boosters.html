What Is It?

Skin boosters are specialized injections determined by physician assessment to improve overall skin quality gradually and sustainably.

Who Is It For?

  • Dry or inelastic skin
  • Dullness and lack of glow
  • General skin quality improvement
  • Uneven texture

Session Steps

  1. 1Precise medical assessment
  2. 2Suitable type selection

  4. 4Aftercare instructions

Benefits

Deep, lasting hydration
Natural radiance
Better texture and elasticity
Healthier, more vibrant appearance

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between skin boosters and filler?
Filler adds volume, while skin boosters improve skin quality from within.
How long do results last?
Between 6–12 months depending on type and condition.
